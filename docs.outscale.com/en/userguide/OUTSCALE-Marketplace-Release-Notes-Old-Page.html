OUTSCALE Marketplace Release Notes (Old Page)

This page lists the new features contained in each release of OUTSCALE Marketplace.

2024

June 17, 2024

As of June 17, 2024, the release notes are located on a new page: Release Notes.

June 3, 2024

  • As a seller partner:

    • The product creation procedure has been updated. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > Product Type Selection and Creating and Publishing an Outsourced Product > Product Type Selection.

  • As a buyer:

    • When subscribing to a subscription offer and reserving one or more virtual machines (VMs), the consumption duration and billing frequency of the offer and reservation are now automatically aligned. For more information, see Subscribing to an OMI Product > Subscription Offer.

May 2, 2024

  • The creation of an organization is now subject to validation. For more information, see Creating an Organization as a Seller Partner and Creating an Organization as a Buyer.

  • As a seller partner, you can now delete your products in draft status. For more information, see Deleting a Draft Product.

  • An OUTSCALE Marketplace FAQ is now available.

April 8, 2024

  • As a seller partner:

    • When creating an OMI product and specifying its general information, you can now publish the product in both the cloudgouv-eu-west-1 and eu-west-2 Regions. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > General Information.

    • When creating and modifying your showcase, you can now specify the association(s) your organization belongs to. For more information, see Creating Your Showcase and Modifying Your Showcase.

    • When creating and modifying a product, you can now specify the URL of a YouTube video. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > General Information, Creating and Publishing an Outsourced Product, and Modifying a Product.

  • As a buyer:

    • You can now deploy OMI products in the cloudgouv-eu-west-1 and eu-west-2 Regions. For more information, see About OUTSCALE Marketplace > OMIs, Estimating the Cost of a Product, and Subscribing To an OMI Product.

    • You can now subscribe several times to an offer. For more information, see Subscribing To an OMI Product.

March 18, 2024

  • As a seller partner:

    • When creating an OMI product and specifying its offer, you can now configure a subscription pricing mode. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > Offer.

  • As a buyer:

    • You can now subscribe to an OMI product on a recurring basis. For more information, see Subscribing To an OMI Product > Subscription Offer.

  • The OUTSCALE Marketplace homepage has been redesigned.

February 20, 2024

  • As a buyer:

    • Information regarding the cost and duration of virtual machine (VM) reservations has been added to the Subscribing To an OMI Product and Estimating the Cost of a Product pages.

February 7, 2024

  • As a seller partner:

    • When creating an OMI product in bring your own license (BYOL) mode, you can now specify the URL to which buyers are redirected to subscribe to the product. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > Offer.

January 24, 2024

  • As a buyer:

    • When subscribing to a product, you can now reserve one or more virtual machines (VMs) among different VM types recommended by the seller partner, and for a Cloud account belonging to your organization. If required, you can also enter a purchase order ID. For more information, see About VMs > Reserved VMs and Subscribing To an OMI Product.

January 2, 2024

  • OUTSCALE Marketplace is no longer in beta.

  • OUTSCALE Marketplace’s URL is now the following: https://marketplace.outscale.com/.

2023

November 28, 2023

  • As a seller partner:

    • As an administrator, you can now add OUTSCALE accounts to your organization to automatically share the permissions to the OMIs your organization subscribes to with these accounts. For more information, see Managing OUTSCALE Accounts as a Seller Partner.

    • When creating or modifying an OMI product, you can now add a template to automate product deployment. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > Deployment Template and Modifying a Product.

  • As a buyer:

    • As an administrator, you can now add OUTSCALE accounts to your organization to automatically share the permissions to the OMIs your organization subscribes to with these accounts. For more information, see Managing OUTSCALE Accounts as a Buyer.

    • Seller partners can now add templates to their OMI products to automate their deployment. You can access deployment templates from the My subscriptions page and from product pages.

    • You can now access product pages from the My subscriptions page.

November 8, 2023

  • As a seller partner:

    • When creating an OMI product and specifying its offer, you can now configure a free/open source billing mode. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > Offer.

    • When creating an OMI product and specifying its offer, you can now define a top virtual machine (VM) recommendation. For more information, see Creating and Publishing an OMI Product > Offer.

    • When creating an OMI product, its product code is now automatically associated.

  • As a buyer:

    • You can now subscribe to free/open source OMI products. For more information, see Subscribing to an OMI Product > Free/Open Source Offer.

    • When estimating the cost of an OMI product and subscribing to an OMI product, you can now select a top VM recommendation made by the seller partner. For more information, see Estimating the Cost of a Product and Subscribing To an OMI Product.

October 18, 2023

  • As a seller partner:

    • You can now modify the legal documents of your OMI products after the publication of the said products. For more information, see Modifying a Product.

    • You can now unpublish OMI product versions because they have reached their end of life or because of a security problem. For more information, see Managing OMI Product Versions > Unpublishing a Version.

    • When the first administrator creates the organization, they must now specify if the company is domiciled in France or abroad. For more information, see Creating an Organization as a Seller Partner.

  • As a buyer:

    • The procedure to subscribe to an outsourced product has been updated. For more information, see Subscribing To an Outsourced Product.

    • Your My subscriptions page is updated, and your organization receives an email, when seller partners modify the legal documents of their OMI products and apply the modifications to existing subscribers. For more information, see Subscribing To an OMI Product.

    • When the first administrator creates the organization, they must now specify if the company domiciled in France or abroad. For more information, see Creating an Organization as a Buyer.

  • You can now submit your seller partner application via the Becoming a seller partner page.

  • EIM resources marked by a marketplace_ prefix in the APIs and Cockpit v1 must not be modified nor deleted for the corresponding Marketplace accounts to be functional. For more information, see About OUTSCALE Marketplace > Administrators and Users.

  • This release also includes design, stability, and accessibility improvements.

September 7, 2023

  • As a seller partner:

    • You can now manage product versions. For more information, see the new procedure Managing OMI Product Versions.

    • The product depublication procedure has been updated. You can now unpublish OMI products because they have reached their end of life. For more information, see Unpublishing a Product.

  • As a buyer:

    • The procedure to subscribe to an OMI product on demand has been updated. For more information, see Subscribing To an OMI Product > Hourly Offer.

    • From the new Product History page, you can now:

      • View your on-demand product subscriptions.

      • View the products in BYOL mode you have accessed via the seller partners' websites.

      For more information, see Subscribing To an OMI Product.

    This release also includes design, stability, and accessibility improvements.

July 17, 2023

  • By default, the first administrator of an organization now has both an administrator role and a user role.

  • When creating an OMI product and completing its Version section, you can now select an OMI name or ID.

  • The My Products page now includes a Last update column.

  • You can now copy the product code of a product across the interface using a dedicated button.

  • This release also includes design and stability improvements.

June 12, 2023

  • The Marketplace documentation has been updated:

    • New procedures are available for seller partners and buyers. For more information, see Seller Partner, Tutorial: Getting Started as a Seller Partner on OUTSCALE Marketplace, and Buyer.

    • For more information about OUTSCALE Marketplace, see About OUTSCALE Marketplace.

2022

August 3, 2022

  • The page documenting OUTSCALE Marketplace has been updated: About OUTSCALE Marketplace.
