Managing Marketplace Accounts as a Buyer

The administrator(s) of your organization can manage Marketplace accounts, that is creating and deleting them, and modifying their information and/or roles. For more information about administrators, see About OUTSCALE Marketplace > Administrators and Users.

Creating a Marketplace Account

  1. In the top right corner of the Marketplace interface, click your username or email address.
    A drop-down menu appears.

  2. Click Marketplace account management.
    The Marketplace account management page appears.

  3. Click Create Marketplace account.
    A form appears.

  4. Enter the first and last name, and email associated to the account.

  5. Select the account’s role, User, Administrator, or both.

    For more information about roles, see About OUTSCALE Marketplace > Administrators and Users.

  6. Click Save.
    The account is created and the corresponding user is sent an email requiring the creation of a password for their account.

Modifying a Marketplace Account

  1. In the top right corner of the Marketplace interface, click your username or email address.
    A drop-down menu appears.

  2. Click Marketplace account management.
    The Marketplace account management page appears.

  3. Click Edit Marketplace account on the line of the account you want to modify.
    A form appears.

  4. Modify the account information.

  5. Click Save.
    The account is modified and the corresponding user is sent an email notifying them of the modification.

Deleting a Marketplace Account

  1. In the top right corner of the Marketplace interface, click your username or email address.
    A drop-down menu appears.

  2. Click Marketplace account management.
    The Marketplace account management page appears.

  3. Click Delete Marketplace account on the line of the account you want to delete.
    A confirmation dialog box appears.

  4. Click Confirm.
    The account is deleted and the corresponding user is sent an email notifying them of their deletion.

    Deleted accounts will not be able to log in to the Marketplace.
